Megashot VR

Megashot VR is a VR game that I am developing for the Oculus Quest. It's a shooter style game set in the near-future where you infiltrate enemy bases, kill guard robots, and steal enemy intelligence.

When you kill high-ranking guards, you get their security keycard so that you can access more parts of the base.

Your hands in the game ARE controllers and when you use a weapon the virtual world tracking ring disappears and is replaces by said weapon.

The right controller will be used to select and use weapons while the left hand will be used to teleport, access your inventory, and use a flashlight. Both hands can grab objects.

Features

  • Realistic reloading
  • Advanced physics
  • A slow-motion ability
  • Multiplayer

I am designing this game for the Oculus Quest because I plan to have intense shootouts where you can duck and hide behing obstacles to avoid fire, and I don't want a cable inhibiting your movement.


Space Defence Fighter 37 (SDF-37)

SDF-37 is the successor to Space Race. I are building this game from the ground up in Unity Engine, overhauling the movement system, removing the "classes" and replacing them with weapon and ability pick-ups.

I am adding multiplayer, campaign mode, AI battlegrounds to test your ability, many more weapons, more abilities, better graphics, and a storyline!
